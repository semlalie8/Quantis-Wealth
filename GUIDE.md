# 📈 Quantis Wealth: Strategic Asset Allocation Guide

Welcome to the **Quantis Wealth Platform**. This guide explains how to leverage our AI-driven ecosystem to manage institutional-grade wealth using modern portfolio theory and reinforcement learning.

---

## 🚀 1. The Core Philosophy
Quantis moves beyond traditional "Buy and Hold" strategies. We utilize **Dynamic Asset Allocation (DAA)**, where the engine continuously learns from market volatility and macroeconomic shifts to adjust your exposure.

### Key Pillars:
- **Modern Portfolio Theory (MPT)**: Optimizing for the "Efficient Frontier" (maximum return for a given level of risk).
- **Reinforcement Learning (RL)**: Our AI agent treats the market as an environment and "learns" which allocation shifts lead to superior Sharpe Ratios over time.

---

## 🛠️ 2. How to Use the Platform

### Step A: Dashboard Analysis
Start every session on your **Dashboard**. 
- **Total Balance & Yield**: Monitor your absolute growth.
- **Performance Chart**: Use the time-period tabs (1W, 1M, 1Y) to distinguish between short-term noise and long-term trends.
- **Allocation Donut**: Visualize your current diversification. Ensure you aren't "over-concentrated" in a single sector (e.g., too much Crypto or too many Equities).

### Step B: Asset & Strategy Management
Before rebalancing, you must define your constraints on the **Allocation** page:
1. **Choose Your Strategy**: Select between **Conservative**, **Moderate**, or **Aggressive**. This changes the "Risk Tolerance" parameter the AI uses to calculate the efficient frontier.
2. **Add New Assets**: Click the **"Add New Asset"** button in the header to incorporate new stocks, crypto, or commodities into your tracking list.
3. **Manage Data Sets**: Scroll down to the **"Data Sources & Sets"** section. Here you can:
    - **Import Datasets**: Click "Import Dataset" to upload custom CSV or JSON price history for unique assets.
    - **Monitor Live Streams**: View the status of live API connections (e.g., Coinbase or Quantis Cloud) that feed the RL engine real-time market data.
    - **Check Engine Health**: Ensure "Learning Consistency" and "Pipeline Latency" are within optimal ranges for institutional analysis.

### Step C: The Smart Rebalance
Use the **Risk Audit** page to deep-dive into your portfolio's "health" metrics:
- **Beta ($\beta$)**: Measure your sensitivity to the broader market.
- **Value at Risk (VaR)**: Understand the maximum likely loss in a single day.
- **Stress Testing**: See how your portfolio would have performed during historical crashes (e.g., 2008 or 2020).

### Step D: AI Insights
Visit the **AI Insights** page to "chat" with the Quantis Engine. You can ask:
- *"Which of my assets has the highest correlation right now?"*
- *"Should I increase my exposure to commodities based on current inflation trends?"*

## 📊 3. Wealth Management Best Practices

1. **Rebalance Monthly**: Markets move fast. We recommend running the Smart Rebalance tool at least once a month to capture "mean reversion" opportunities.
2. **Diversify Across Classes**: Don't just pick stocks. Use the **Allocation** page to balance between Equities, Crypto, Fixed Income, and Commodities.
3. **Monitor the Sharpe Ratio**: A high return is only good if the risk was low. Always aim for a Sharpe Ratio above 1.0.
